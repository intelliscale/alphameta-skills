# Industry Overview / 行业概览

Generates a comprehensive industry or sector panorama report covering market dynamics, competitive landscape, key players, and trends.

## Current Status

AlphaMeta does not provide industry-level data aggregation or sector classification. Industry overview reports require:

- Industry classification mapping (GICS / ICB / SW)
- Peer group financial comparison
- Sector-level valuation data

**Fallback**: Use WebSearch to research industry structure and competitive landscape. Combine with individual symbol data via `quote`, `consensus`, and `financial-report` for key players.
