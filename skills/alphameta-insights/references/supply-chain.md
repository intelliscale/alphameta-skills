# Supply Chain / 产业链

Maps upstream, midstream, and downstream value chain for a target industry — identifies bottlenecks, margin concentration, and investment opportunities.

## Current Status

Supply chain analysis is a framework-only capability — it requires industry knowledge and multi-company financial comparison that AlphaMeta does not directly support.

**Approach**: Use WebSearch for industry knowledge and supply chain mapping. For individual companies in the identified chain, use AlphaMeta commands (`quote`, `financial-report`, `consensus`, `operating`) to verify financial health and margin trends.

No automated supply-chain mapping is available via the current API.
